Consequences of using AI in political campaigns

How does using AI in political campaigns affect our society?

Krenbah, Wafaa. - 02.10.2023

Potential Positive consequences

Improved election fairness: The proposed law can make elections fairer by reducing the use of AI-generated misinformation, ensuring that all candidates play by the same rules.

Increased public confidence: It can boost people's trust in elections, as they'll have more faith that campaign information is honest and not manipulated by AI.

Lower risk of deception: Implementing the law can reduce the chances of voters being tricked by AI-generated content, making elections more trustworthy and transparent.
